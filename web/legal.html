Photo attribution

All listing photos shown on pulpo.club are sourced from publicly accessible broker listing pages. Every listing links prominently to its original source so the broker's page can be viewed directly. pulpo.club does not claim ownership of any listing photos and displays them solely to help buyers identify properties of interest.

Aggregator notice

pulpo.club is a listing aggregator. We do not own, manage, or sell any of the real estate inventory displayed. All prices, areas, and availability are reported from third-party broker sources and may not reflect current conditions. Always verify details directly with the listing broker before making any investment decision.

Takedown requests

If you are a broker or rights holder and would like a specific listing or photo removed from pulpo.club, please contact us at legal@pulpo.club. We commit to acknowledging takedown requests within 48 hours and removing the content within 7 business days of a valid request.

Please include in your request: the listing URL on pulpo.club, the original source URL, and your relationship to the content (broker, photographer, or rights holder).
